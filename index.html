MISSION

To provide intelligent and energy-efficient appliance solutions that help consumers reduce electricity consumption, protect appliances from electrical damage, monitor and remotely control appliances and promote sustainable energy practices through innovative hardware and software technology.

VISION

To become a leading smart energy technology company in the Philippines and globally by transforming ordinary appliances into intelligent, safe, and energy-efficient systems for homes and businesses. VoltKo aims to become the pavement for future smart appliance technologies and push forward innovations with smart appliance technology.

WHAT IS VOLTKO?

Voltko is a technology-based company focused on developing smart energy management software and hardware for household and commercial appliances. The company partners with appliance manufacturers to integrate intelligent automation, energy monitoring, and electrical protection systems directly into appliances.

Voltko aims to improve energy efficiency, reduce electricity costs, and protect appliances from electrical damage caused by power outages, surges, and hardware faults. Through desktop and mobile applications, users can monitor and manage their appliance more efficiently.

The company promotes innovation, sustainability, and smart living by combining artificial intelligence, automation, appliance monitoring and energy-saving technologies.

OBJECTIVES

Voltko aims to develop an AI-powered smart appliance software system that helps consumers manage electricity usage efficiently while protecting appliances from electrical problems such as power surges and outages. The project also focuses on providing real-time monitoring, intelligent automation, and energy-saving features through a mobile and desktop application.

  • To design and implement an appliance-integrated smart software platform for monitoring electricity consumption.
  • To provide automated appliance protection features against power outages, voltage fluctuations, and electrical surges.
  • To develop intelligent energy efficiency management using sensors and AI-powered automation.
  • To create a desktop and mobile application that allows users to monitor and manage appliance energy usage in real time.
  • To help consumers reduce electricity expenses and improve appliance lifespan through smart technology.
